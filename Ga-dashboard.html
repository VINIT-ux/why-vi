A smart, safety-focused tool that helps pilots and crew manage flights, track data,
and stay connected‚Äîon the ground and in the air.

Design Brief

The goal was to design a comprehensive yet user-friendly dashboard tailored for general aviation pilots. The dashboard needed to centralize essential information like flight planning, aircraft status, real time weather, and communication tools‚Äîall while maintaining a clean, accessible interface suitable for high-pressure aviation environments.

Key objectives included: Simplify pre- and post-flight tasks Show real-time data and alerts Improve team coordination Highlight safety-critical info Support desktop and tablet use Build for future expansion (training, analytics)

The final product had to support tablet interfaces, adhere to aviation safety standards, and be adaptable for future features like training modules and fleet analytics.

01 - My Design Approach

I followed a user-centered design process starting with research, mapping key workflows, and validating ideas through wireframes and usability testing. The goal was to balance functionality with simplicity, ensuring the interface supports real-time decision-making without overwhelming users.

I began with user research to understand workflows and pain points, then created wireframes to map key tasks. Through usability testing, I refined the design to ensure clarity and ease of use. A modular design system supported consistency, with a strong focus on speed, safety, and real-time decision-making.

Dashboard Overview

The Home Dashboard offers a quick snapshot of flight status and key metrics. Flight Details provides route, speed, and altitude data with real-time updates. Fuel & Engine Stats display critical performance indicators, while the Communication Panel keeps pilots connected with simplified controls.

Alerts & Notifications highlight urgent updates, and Maintenance Logs track system health and service needs. Finally, the Settings screen enables personalization for a more intuitive and efficient user experience.

Flight Details

The Flight Details page presents real-time route, altitude, speed, and ETA in a clear, visual layout‚Äîhelping pilots stay informed and make quick decisions with minimal distraction.

Post-Flight Insights

Summarizes key performance metrics like average speed, fuel consumption, and engine behavior helping pilots and crews reflect, optimize future flights, and ensure aircraft health.

Maintenance Page

The Maintenance page tracks system status, schedules, and recent issues making it easy to monitor aircraft health, log checks, and stay ahead of potential problems.

System Settings

The System Settings page allows users to customize themes, units, and display preferences‚Äîensuring a personalized and comfortable dashboard experience.

On-Cruise

The On-Cruise page provides a focused view of altitude, speed, engine status, and weather‚Äîdesigned to support smooth monitoring during stable flight phases.
